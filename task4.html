    • Rajeev Gandhi Memorial College of Engineering and Technology was founded in the year 1995. It is located in a 32.04 acre sprawling campus on NH-40 (old NH-18) at Nandyal, , Andhra Pradesh. It is the dedicated commitment and efforts of our Chairman, the man with vision "Vidyarathna" Dr. M. Santhiramudu, who started the institution with a motto "EDUCATION FOR PEACE". RGMCET is a road of elegant educational journey, yet path breaking in different dimensions. GMCET encourges a lot of outside-the-curriculum activities to keepthe students motivated and drive them towards success.
