    • Army School Kandrori was bifurcated from Little Angle’s School Kandrori and was inaugurated as an independent Army School on 11 April 1994 and now enjoying the status of Army Public School from the year 2010. Little Angles School Kandrori was established on 22 March 1985 as a unit run school, looked after by 9 FOD with classes Nursery to V.

    After its inauguration as an Army School in the 1994, one class was added every year and 1st batch of class – X students appeared in All India Secondary School Examination in 1999.The school had +2 Humanities stream from 2004 – 2007. The school has started class XI- Science stream from academic session 2015-16. At present the school has only science stream from session 2015-16.In 20__ the Army School has been renamed as Army Public School Kandrori.

    The school started with a strength of approximately 500 students and now it is a big family which includes 1100 plus students nearly 46 teachers, 04 Non-teaching and 13 Adm staff.

    With the dedicated team of educators and enthusiastic learners , responsive management and cooperative parents the school is continuously growing and evading into a fine educational institute

    The school is affiliated to the Central Board of Secondary Education, New Delhi vide affiliation No 1630241 and regular affiliation granted by CBSE vide New affiliation No 1680020 and the curriculum formulated by the board is strictly followed. Extra coaching classes for academically weaker students are regularly conducted to enhance the overall academic standard of the school. The introduction of zero period has helped the students in achieving the desired academic standard and also gave them ample opportunity to hone their skills in various co-curricular activities and the school has been successful in fulfilling both the objectives..

    The aim of education is overall personality development of students. We have made co-curricular activities an integral part of the school curriculum. Various inter-house, and inter-school activities like debate, calligraphy, painting quiz, badminton, football, basketball etc are conducted throughout the year.
